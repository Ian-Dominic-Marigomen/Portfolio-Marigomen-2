The National Flag of India (Hindi:Tiranga) is a horizontal rectangular tricolour of India saffron, white and India green;with the Ashok Chakra, a 24-spoke wheel, in navy blue at it's centre

The National Flag of Paraguay is a red-white-blue triband, was inspired by the colours of the French of the French Tricolour, believed to signify independence and liberty
The flag is unusual because it differs on it's obverse (front) and reverse (back)
the obverse of the flag shows the national coat of arms, and the reverse shows the seal of the treasury
